# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Indonesians checking exchange rates before transferring money — predominantly on mobile,
in transit or at the bank. They have a specific job: get the rate, confirm the math, move on.
Secondary: expats and travelers with the same job in different currency pairs.

## Product Purpose
Standalone SEO-driven currency converter targeting Indonesian users. Organic traffic via
static currency pair pages (/convert/[pair]). Homepage is the identity anchor; convert pages
are the revenue surface (indexed, shareable, linkable). Success = ranking for high-intent
queries like "USD to IDR" and "SGD to IDR".

## Brand Personality
Trusted, sharp, unhurried. The brand feels like a confident expert who gives you the
number without ceremony. Not a fintech startup, not a bank — a precise instrument.

## Anti-references
- Xe.com: dated UI, information overload, anxiety-inducing density
- Generic fintech SaaS: teal-to-green gradients, rounded blob illustrations, "Money, simplified" headlines
- Flashy crypto: neon-on-dark, massive numbers, speculation energy
- Google Finance: functional but soulless, zero brand presence

## Design Principles
1. Rate first — the exchange rate is the product; nothing competes with it in hierarchy
2. Speed as respect — pages feel instant; no loading theater, no skeleton abuse
3. Calm authority — settled type, no urgency, the data is enough
4. Mobile by default — every layout decision starts from 375px thumb reach
5. Less surface, more signal — strip anything that isn't information or action

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Mobile-first. Respect prefers-reduced-motion where animations are used.
